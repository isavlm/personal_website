A versatile professional with a bachelor’s degree in social communication from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte and a Master's degree in Strategic Communications from Trinity Washington University, and a certificate in Digital Marketing and SEO from COOP Careers. Her expertise includes creating and producing digital content on various platforms Isadora has contributed as a writer and photographer to Prost Amerika, covering both local and international soccer. During 2016-2018, she covered D.C. United and provided Twitter coverage for MLS, Copa Centenario (2016), and the Men's FIFA World Cup (2018). She recently served as an Implementation Assistant at the Inter-American Development Bank, managing and editing 16 online courses on edX. She thrives on challenges and continuously hones her skills. She has a penchant for learning computer languages on the fly, developing a portfolio website, and practicing photography on both iPhones and DSLRs. She enjoys playing video games, learning about dog training, practicing Kung Fu, or teaming up with her husband on nerdy projects in her spare time.
